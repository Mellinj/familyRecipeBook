Sibonet's Alfredo

Favorite Pizza Crust

Recipe:

Sibonet's Alfredo Sauce:

  • 8 oz. Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 c. Butter
  • 3/4 c. Parm Cheese
  • 1/2 c. Milk

    • Melt together and serve over your favorite pasta.

    Favorite Pizza Crust:

  • 2 1/2 tsp. Yeast
  • 1 c. Warm Water
  • 2 1/2 c. Flour
  • 2 tsp. Honey
  • 1 tsp. Salt
  • 2 T. Oil

    • Preheat overn to 400F. Bake crust for 8 - 10 min. Place favorite topping and bake for another 10 - 12 min. Makes enough for 1 cookie sheet.
