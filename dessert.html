No Bake PB Bars

  • 1 c. Melted Butter
  • 1 c. Peanut Butter
  • 2 c. Graham Crackers
  • 2 c. Pwd. Sugar

    • Melt together and place in bottom of an 8X10 pan.

  • 1 1/2 c. Chocolate Chips
  • 4 T. Peanut Butter

    • Melt together and place on top of the Peanut Butter mixture. Let it harden in the fridge for approx. 2 hrs then cut and serve.

    Rice Krispy Treats

  • 6 T. Butter
  • 6 c. Marshmallows
  • 6 c. Rice Cereal

    • Sugar Cookies

  • 2/3 c. Shortening
  • 2/3 c. Butter
  • 1 1/2 c. White Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tsp. Vanilla
  • 3 1/2 c. Flour
  • 2 tsp. Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp. Salt

    • Preheat oven to 350F. Cream together butter, shortening, and sugar. Then mix in eggs and vanilla. Add dry ingredients. Bake 8 - 10 minutes.

    Ellie's Great Rolls

  • 1 1/2 T. Yeast
  • 1/2 T. Sugar
  • 1/2 c. Water
  • 4 1/2 - 5 c. Flour
  • 1/4 c. Sugar
  • 1/2 T. Salt
  • 1 1/2 c. Warm Water
  • 1/4 c. Oil

    • 1. In a large bowl add 4.5 - 5 cups of flour, 1/4 c. sugar, and 1/2 T of salt. Mix and add yeast mixture, 1 1/2 cup warm water and 1/4 cup of oil. Mix well and let it rest for 30 min.

    2. Knead and let it raise for 1 - 2 hrs as needed. Bake at 375F for 20 - 35 min.
